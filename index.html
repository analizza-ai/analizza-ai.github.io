§ 02 / Advisory

Acquisition advisory for active capital.

When a deal is large, contested, or atypical, the model is a starting point — not a verdict. Our team pairs the product output with hands-on deal work, end to end.

Independent diligence, from sourcing to close.

We are retained by funds, family offices, and high-conviction individuals to underwrite real estate acquisitions where the downside of being wrong is meaningful. Engagements are scoped to your thesis — never templated.

    Every shortlisted asset goes through legal, valuation, and construction review with senior analyst sign-off.
